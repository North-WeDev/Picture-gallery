About us

Welcome to Pictobox, the webapp that bring your photos to life! Expeience the best, experience - Pictobox. The name Pictobox comes from the Legend of Zelda series: namely from The Windwaker. We believe nature should be the ultimate source of relaxation. The various colours presented in our pictures will enable you to see things differently.
